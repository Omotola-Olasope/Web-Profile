Hello World!

I'm Arunn, a Ma|






I'm a Senior Data Scientist with 4 years of industry experience.

My mission is to inspire enthusiasts to break in and then grow in the world of data science! Currently, I work at OCTAVE - John Keells Holdings, where I solve impactful business problems for the Retail sector by unlocking the power of data.

Before joining OCTAVE, I worked at Trabeya - An AI startup, where I was designing, developing, and deploying end-to-end AI products for business problems in the domain of Smart Cities, Data Privacy, Genomics, Aquaculture, and more.

Additionally, I write articles on Data Science & Machine Learning, build pet-projects, invest time in continual development through MOOCs, and make a conscious effort to share the knowledge.

Summing it all up, I learn, create and share.
